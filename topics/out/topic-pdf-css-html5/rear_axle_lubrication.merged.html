Rear Axle Lubrication Requirements

Proper lubrication procedures and maintenance schedules for the truck rear axle differential and bearings.

Lubricant Specifications

Use A-1 heavy fluid or semi-fluid oil such as:

  • Mobiloil C
  • Whittemore's Worm Gear Protective

Maintain oil level even with upper oil plug.

Table 1. Maintenance Schedule
MileageRequired Maintenance
500 milesFirst oil change
1000 milesSecond oil change
Every 100 milesTurn outer bearing dope cups one full turn

Post-Service Procedure

  1. Fill differential with oil
  2. Jack up axle
  3. Run for 5-10 minutes to ensure proper bearing lubrication
